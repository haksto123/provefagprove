<summary> How To Create/Edit/Delete Quizes </summary>

- You can only create, edit, and delete quizzes if you have the proper permissions.
If you only have user role the "edit quizes" tab will not show up.
- To create a quiz you will have to go in the edit quizes tab, then write the name of your quiz, add a description, and choose a category from the dropdown
  <img width="985" height="568" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/7872006f-ec93-47f2-aaf9-f24451da69af" />

- Then you can add a question by writing in the Question Text field, and you can choose how much you can the score to increase/decrease by on each question (default value is 100 points increase and -50 decrease)

  <img width="1079" height="289" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/8d22b664-a84e-4578-ad35-f02cf32e1bc6" />


- At last you can create corresponding answers and choose which answer should be correct in the grid below questions
  <img width="1093" height="711" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/443cfaa0-dabd-4645-b74e-322d1b617034" />

- To edit quizes you simply have to click in the row of the quiz/question/answer you wanna edit.
  
- To delete a quiz/question/answer you have to click the "x" on the right side of the grid 
  <img width="906" height="86" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/f0c659ae-4795-4f4d-87a2-96732143f9f8" />

- When you have created your quiz it will appear in the Dashboard Tab

<summary> How To Play </summary>

- In the Quiz Dashboard tab you can filter on categories to see whatever quizes you prefer.

  <img width="1252" height="521" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/c0196bc5-642f-4fcf-a604-8856afeb28e3" />
  <img width="672" height="393" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/85a583b2-5495-4b69-9dfe-584282e60414" />

  
- Click the "Start Quiz" button to start.
- You'll be sent to a page where the quiz starts
  <img width="1303" height="538" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/8c7b1b41-719d-4c70-8fc2-7c21b9ea0c51" />

- Click an answer then click submit answer, you'll then see if you got the question right or wrong.
- Your point score will increase with every right question you get, aswell as it will decrease if you get a question wrong

  <img width="1311" height="725" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/011dcd67-510f-4fe0-bf50-18d6c17ae916" />

- At the end the button changes to "See Result" and it will take you to another page where you can see your final score, aswell as retry the quiz or go back to dashboard
- You also have the option to view a PDF file of you final score

  <img width="1456" height="660" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/694e2950-3dcd-4f94-b6c4-b3907329c7cb" />

  <img width="717" height="685" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/0ea9ccb2-427b-41ec-a5e0-2c40959a3cff" />

  <img width="1148" height="548" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/9b758661-f4b7-4c8f-94da-917f64abe306" />

- You can then view all the attemts in the scoreboard section, you can also filter by Created to see your most recent attempt
- Normally it will sort by score to see which person has the best score

  <img width="730" height="1169" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/3eb1a8ce-9519-4dc8-8198-1db8b249489e" />
